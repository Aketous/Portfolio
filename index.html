About me

I'm a Network & Security Engineer based in Marrakech, holding a Professional Master's in Systems, Networks & Security Engineering from ENSI Tangier.

Since 2021 I've worked at Axians–Cegelec, deploying and maintaining enterprise networks end to end — from active/passive Wi-Fi site surveys with Ekahau, to configuring Catalyst 9000 switches, Catalyst 9800 (WLC) wireless controllers and Catalyst 9100 access points, hardening networks with Fortinet firewalls & SD-WAN, and virtualizing servers on VMware ESXi.

I care about networks that are fast, resilient and secure by design — and I enjoy the puzzle of optimizing coverage, performance and reliability for real users.
